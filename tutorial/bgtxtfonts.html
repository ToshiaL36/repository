There are generic typefaces that work across most browsers and platforms. These families are: - serif – normal fonts with serifs such as Garamond, Georgia, New York, Times, Times New Roman - sans-serif – normal fonts without serifs such as Arial, Geneva, Helvetica, Lucida Sans, Trebuchet, Verdana - monospace – fixed-width fonts such as Courier, Consolas, Lucida Console, Monaco - cursive – fonts that emulate handwriting such as Comic Sans MS, Lucida Handwriting, Zapf Chancery - fantasy – decorative fonts such as Copperplate, Desdemona, Impact, Kino
